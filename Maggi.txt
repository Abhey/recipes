Ingredients:
 - 1 Packet of Maggi
 - 1 Glass of Water

Directions:
 - 1 Add water to a bowl and heat it for 1 minutes.
 - 2 Add Maggi masala to it and let the water heat for another 
     1 minute.
 - 3 Add Maggi Noodles to the bowl and wait for alteast 5  
     minutes untill water is almost dried up.
 - 4 Now your Maggi is ready to serve.
